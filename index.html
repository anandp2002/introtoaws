What are cloud service providers (CSPs) ?

Cloud service providers (CSPs) are companies that offer various cloud computing services and infrastructure resources to individuals, businesses, and organizations. These providers own and operate data centers with extensive computing resources, storage, and networking infrastructure, which they make available to their customers over the internet.


Here are some well-known cloud service providers

1 . Amazon Web Services (AWS): AWS, a subsidiary of Amazon.com, is one of the leading cloud service providers. It offers a comprehensive suite of cloud services, including computing power, storage, databases, analytics, machine learning, networking, and more.

2 . Microsoft Azure: Azure is the cloud computing platform provided by Microsoft. It offers a wide range of cloud services, including virtual machines, databases, AI services, analytics, networking, and developer tools.

3 . Google Cloud Platform (GCP): GCP is the cloud computing platform provided by Google. It offers cloud services such as computing, storage, machine learning, data analytics, and networking. GCP also includes services like Google Cloud AI and Google Kubernetes Engine.

4 . IBM Cloud: IBM Cloud provides a suite of cloud services and solutions, including infrastructure as a service (IaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), software as a service (SaaS), AI, blockchain, analytics, and more.

5 . Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI)

6 . Alibaba Cloud
