Step 1 : Create an AWS account

1 . Go to the AWS homepage: Open a web browser and visit the AWS homepage at https://aws.amazon.com/

2 . On the AWS homepage, click on the "Create an AWS Account" button. It is usually located in the top-right corner of the page.

3 . Enter your email address and AWS account name in the designated field and click on the "Verify email address" button.

4 . Enter the verification code recieved in your mail and click Verify

5 . Enter a strong password and click continue

6 . Enter your contact information and check the small box to agree to their terms and conditions after reading it. Then click continue

7 . Enter your card details (Rupay card can't be used) and click "Verify and continue"

8 . In this step, it will take you to the payment gateway to validate your payment information and for your credit/debit card verification, Amazon will charge the minimum price based on Country. Here I have provided India, so Amazon charged 2 INR.

9 . Confirm your identity. Basically, you need to select a mode to confirm your identity. It could be a Text Message or a Voice call to your valid phone number.

10 . In the next step for creating an AWS Account, we need to select the plan for our AWS Account. Unless you are planning to do some professional development, I would suggest selecting the Basic Plan. It is Free of cost and great for learning purposes.

Once you select your plan, you will see the below Welcome screen. From here on, you can Sign in to your AWS Console.
